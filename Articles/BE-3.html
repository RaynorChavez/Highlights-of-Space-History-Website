BE-3 - January 2013

The BE-3 (Blue Engine 3) is a LH2/LOXrocket engine developed by Blue Origin.The engine began development in the early 2010s, and completed acceptance testing in early 2015. The engine is being used on the Blue Origin New Shepardsuborbital rocket, for which test flights began in 2015. The engine is under consideration by United Launch Alliance (ULA) for use in a new second stage, the Advanced Cryogenic Evolved Stage, in ULA's Vulcanorbital launch vehicle with first flight in the 2020s.
